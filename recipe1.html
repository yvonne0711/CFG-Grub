Classic Beef Lasagne

A delicious homemade lasagne with rich beef ragu, creamy béchamel and golden cheese topping. A comfort food classic.

Total time: 60 minutes Servings: 8

Ingredients:

  • Meat Sauce:
    • 1 tbsp olive oil |
    • 1 onion - peeled and finely diced |
    • 2 cloves garlic - peeled and minced |
    • 1 carrot - peeled and finely diced |
    • 3 chestnut (brown) mushrooms - finely diced |
    • 500g minced beef (ground beef) |
    • 3 tbsp tomato puree |
    • 1 tsp dried oregano |
    • 1⁄2 tsp dried thyme |
    • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce |
    • 1 beef stock cube - crumbled |
    • 800g tinned chopped tomatoes |
    • 1⁄2 tbsp light brown sugar |
    • 1⁄2 salt |
    • 1⁄2 tsp black pepper |
  • Béchamel Sauce:
    • 4 tbsp butter - salted or unsalted is fine
    • 4 tbsp plain/all-purpose flour
    • 720ml (3 cups) full fat milk
  • Additional:
    • 12 sheets of fried lasagne
    • 175g (1 3/4 cups) mixed grated cheese
